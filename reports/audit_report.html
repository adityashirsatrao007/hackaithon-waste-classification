🏷️ Mislabel Analysis

71.5% of training images are mislabeled. The filename convention {true_label}_{random_id}.jpg reveals the correct ground truth. The 3LC starter kit deliberately placed images in wrong folders to test data-centric AI skills.

✅ Recommended Fixes

  1. Relabel by filename — use {true_label} prefix from the filename to assign correct class
  2. Remove duplicates — deduplicate by MD5 hash, keep one copy per group
  3. Remove or sharpen blurry — discard images with Laplacian var < 100 or apply sharpening
  4. Augment trash class — oversample or apply aggressive augmentation to balance classes
  5. Cross-validation — use stratified k-fold to mitigate imbalance during training
